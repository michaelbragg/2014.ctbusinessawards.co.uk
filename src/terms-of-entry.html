Terms of Entry

All entries must relate to activity during 2013. Where appropriate, applications are welcomed and encouraged from businesses of all sizes, educational establishments and other public bodies. There are separate entry criteria for each of the Award categories, award sponsors are prohibited from entering their own sponsored category.

The Awards are open to all companies and organisations with a significant presence within the Coventry and Warwickshire Region. However, applications will also be considered from businesses based on the fringe of the Coventry and Warwickshire area, as long as a significant interest within the area can be demonstrated, eg customer base, workforce, etc.

The Awards will assign a judging panel with an appropriate Chairperson. Every effort will be made to give feedback to entrants, although no specific correspondence will be entered into. The decision of the judging panel for each Award and on all associated matters is final.

Coventry Telegraph, Trinity Mirror group companies, Coventry and Warwickshire Chamber of Commerce and selected Coventry Telegraph Business Awards sponsors may contact you and your nominated companies by email, phone or letter with details of relevant goods and services you may be interested in. You will not be charged for these messages.

One entry form, is the basis for entry into multiple awards categories, see form for more details.

Individual companies and organisations may offer only one entry per category.

Every effort will be made to give feedback to entrants although no specific correspondence will be entered into. The decision of the judging panel for each Award and on all associated matters is final.
