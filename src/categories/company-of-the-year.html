Company of the Year

This award is not open to direct entries as the winner of this award will be chosen by the judges from all of the category winners. The Company of the Year Award is to find the overall outstanding company for 2014. The judges will pay particular attention to:

  • Sustained financial growth
  • Number of new permanent employees in the last 12 months
  • Turnaround of a failing or slow growing organisation
  • Development of market leading products or services
  • Growth of productivity and efficiency
  • Pioneering in new markets
  • Commitment to the wider West Midlands region
