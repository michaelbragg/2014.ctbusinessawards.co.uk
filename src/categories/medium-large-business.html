Medium to Large Business

This award is to recognise medium to large businesses that has continued to develop its brand and identity in the Coventry & Warwickshire area.

Eligibility

This category award is open to any company with more than 100 full time staff as of Friday 31st January 2014 within the Coventry and Warwickshire area.

Award sponsor employees are not eligible for this award.

Judging Criteria

The judges will pay particular attention to:

  • All round business excellence
  • Example of strategies that have driven business growth
  • Sustained financial growth
  • Innovation of market leading products or services
  • Growth of productivity and efficiency
  • New market development
  • Commitment to the region

General

All financial information will be treated in the strictest of confidence and will not be disclosed to anyone other than the panel of judges and adviser's. Please provide enough relevant information to enable the assessors to form a clear picture of your achievements.

All submissions to be received by Friday 12th April 2013.
